Samosa

Description

The traditional beef Samosa is among the most preferred breakfast dish in most homes. This article provides both the ingredients and procedures that would ensure you have the most delicious Samosa ever!


Ingredients

    1 pound lean ground beef
    1 medium onion, finely chopped
    2 garlic cloves, crushed
    2 teaspoons ground coriander
    1 ½ teaspoons ground cumin
    ½ teaspoon salt
    ½ teaspoon ground turmeric
    ½ teaspoon chile powder, or more to taste
    ¼ teaspoon ground ginger
    1 teaspoon garam masala
    1 (6 ounce) package frozen peas
    ½ cup water
    1 tablespoon minced pickled jalapeno pepper
    1 tablespoon lemon juice
    1 (12 ounce) package wonton wrappers
    vegetable oil for frying
